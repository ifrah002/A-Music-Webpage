AUR is a Pakistani R&B and hip hop band from Karachi. Their music is a unique blend of these genres, and they are known for their soulful tunes and electrifying energy.

Band members:

Ahad Khan: A childhood friend of Usama Ali, and a writer, poet, lyricist, and vocalist
Usama Ali: A childhood friend of Ahad Khan, and a writer, poet, lyricist, and vocalist
Raffey Anwar: A producer who oversees production and promotions

Notable songs:

Tu Hai Kahan: Their breakthrough single, which has over 58 million YouTube views and 80 million Spotify streams

Jeet Ki Awaz:

A new single from the band

Collaborations:

AUR collaborated with British singer Zayn Malik on their hit song "Tu Hai Kahan". This was the first time a Pakistani band had collaborated with a British singer.
