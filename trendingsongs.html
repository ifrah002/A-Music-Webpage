Kahani Suno


Artist: Kaifi Khalil
Language: Urdu
Meaning: "Tell me a story"
Release: The original version was released in 2021, while the widely popular "Kahani Suno 2.0" was released in 2022.
Popularity: The song topped charts in Pakistan and Bangladesh, gaining significant traction in India as well.
Theme: The lyrics narrate a story of falling in love, expressing deep emotions and personal confessions.
Impact: "Kahani Suno" is considered one of the most successful Pakistani songs on streaming platforms, with its music video reaching the top 10 global charts on YouTube.
Kahani Suno is a popular Urdu song by Pakistani singer Kaifi Khalil, meaning "Tell me a story" in English; it gained widespread recognition after its re-release as "Kahani Suno 2.0" in 2022, becoming a viral hit across South Asia, particularly noted for its heartfelt lyrics about love and longing, sung with a soulful voice and accompanied by an emotional melody.

Teri Baton Me


"Teri Baaton Mein Aisa Uljha Jiya" is a song from the 2024 Bollywood movie of the same name. The song is about an impossible love story between a human and a robot, and features Shahid Kapoor and Kriti Sanon.
Song details: The song was composed by Tanishk Bagchi and Raghav, and the lyrics were written by Tanishk Bagchi and Nina Mathur. The song was sung by Raghav, Tanishk Bagchi, and Asees Kaur.
The song was choreographed by Jani Master.
The song is available on JioSaavn, Gaana, and YouTube.
Movie details: The movie is about a robot scientist who develops feelings for a highly intelligent female robot.
The movie explores the uncharted territories of Artificial Intelligence (AI).
Kriti Sanon plays the role of Sifra, a Super Intelligent Female Robot Automation (SIFRA).
Shahid Kapoor plays the role of the robot scientist.
