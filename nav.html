AUR

The members of the Pakistani R&B and hip hop band AUR are Ahad Khan, Usama Ali, and Raffey Anwar. The band is based in Karachi.

Collaboration with Zayn Malik

AUR collaborated with Zayn Malik on the song "Tu Hai Kahan".
The song was a remake of a previously released song.
Malik, who was previously a member of One Direction, said he was "incredibly humbled" when AUR reached out to him.
